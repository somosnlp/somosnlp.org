To encourage different skills within our community, this year we want to give you the opportunity to design the hackathon poster. The hackathon's goals are the creation of a quality Spanish corpus that includes the varieties of this rich language spoken by 600M people and the creation of a leaderboard that allows us to evaluate LLMs in Spanish. Show your most artistic side and help us create the poster!

## 🎨 How to participate

1. Design a poster (or several)
2. [Send it to us](https://forms.gle/iJrKZtBXvnAk5Ji38) before March 13
3. We will publicly acknowledge all proposals (if you want, of course)
4. If you win, your poster will be the image of the 2025 hackathon!

## ✅ What the poster must include

- Dimensions: 1280 × 720 minimum with 16:9 aspect ratio (landscape)
- Title: Hackathon SomosNLP 2025
- Edition: IV
- Date: March 17 to April 13, 2025
- Website: somosnlp.org/hackathon
- #HackathonSomosNLP
- [SomosNLP Logo](/images/patrocinios/somos_nlp.png)

## 📝 Notes

- Creations must be original
- The use of all types of design tools is allowed, including AI systems
- Transparency regarding the use of AI will be required
- Everyone's participation will be publicly acknowledged

We look forward to your posters with excitement! 🤩
