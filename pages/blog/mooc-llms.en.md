All UPM MOOC courses are online, asynchronous, and free.

## Course duration
50 hours (10 weeks).

## Course description
A course to build a solid technical foundation in generative AI, understand and adapt Large Language Models (LLMs), and apply them safely.

## Course objectives
- Describe what artificial intelligence, generative artificial intelligence, and other types of artificial intelligence are.
- Describe the technical operation and architecture of LLMs.
- Integrate LLMs and LLM-based tools into professional and daily activities.
- Deploy and maintain an LLM for subsequent use.
- Limit and expand the knowledge of LLMs through RAG systems.
- Train a model starting from a base LLM (finetuning).
- Integrate LLMs into external services through web applications.
- Evaluate the quality, energy consumption, and environmental impact of LLMs.
 
## Who is this course for?
This course is aimed at students, professionals, and technology enthusiasts who have basic knowledge of Python programming and web application development. The course is especially designed for those who wish to explore and master advanced techniques related to LLMs, including their use, deployment, fine-tuning, and evaluation in different contexts.

## Recommended prior knowledge
Basic knowledge of Python programming and web application development. 

## Technical requirements
Internet connection.

## Assessment system
At the beginning of the course, a test will be administered so that participants can evaluate their prior knowledge about what they will learn in the course. This preliminary questionnaire will be multiple choice; participants will have a single attempt, and upon completion, the correct answers will be shown. Throughout the modules, students will complete small practical exercises that will be automatically assessed through questionnaires or tools such as AutoCOREctor (already used in other UPM MOOCs).
