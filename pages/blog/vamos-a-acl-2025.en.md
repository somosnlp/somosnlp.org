## 💡 Birds-of-a-Feather session summary

In this session, we will present SomosNLP, the community dedicated to creating and sharing resources that drive and accelerate the development of NLP in Ibero-American languages. Ibero-America refers to the countries of the Americas where Spanish or Portuguese is spoken, and also includes Spain and Portugal. Our goal is to connect with people in the community to collaboratively explore the current challenges in the field. Our aim is to create a dynamic space for sharing knowledge, learning from diverse experiences, and designing innovative solutions together. We want you to join us in this mission! People who speak less-represented languages are especially welcome to join and contribute to this multilingual and exciting dialogue. We look forward to meeting you! ✨
