We are an international community where everyone is welcome regardless of their prior knowledge of NLP. If you are interested in this exciting field, join us and help us in our mission to democratize NLP in Spanish. How?

###  🌱 I'm just starting to learn about NLP

- Follow us and give visibility to our initiatives, articles, and events on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/somosnlp) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/somosnlp_)
- Share events, interests, and projects with the community on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/my8w7JUxZR)
- Help us organize events, design posters, and create content, [contact us](mailto:info@somosnlp.org)
- Help us maintain and improve the [website](https://github.com/somosnlp/somosnlp.org/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#-contribuir-al-desarrollo-de-la-p%C3%A1gina-web)
- Keep learning with our [course](https://somosnlp.org/nlp-de-cero-a-cien) and other [resources](https://somosnlp.org/recursos)


### 🚀 I'm developing my first projects

- We encourage you to create datasets and train open language models:
    - If you get stuck, don't worry, ask us in the #ask-for-help channel
    - If you're looking for a team to develop a project, post your proposal in the #find-a-team channel
- If you discover new open datasets or models in Spanish, add them to the [resources](https://somosnlp.org/recursos) lists
- If while studying you create notes or translate tutorials from English, [share them](https://github.com/somosnlp/somosnlp.org/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#-publicar-un-art%C3%ADculo-en-el-blog) with the community
- Keep learning with the [talks and workshops](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLTA-KAy8nxaCGGYz5CWiLZNzc31ilPDyI) on more advanced topics


### 💡 I'd like to share my experience in the field

- Publish an article or tutorial on our [blog](https://github.com/somosnlp/somosnlp.org/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md#-publicar-un-art%C3%ADculo-en-el-blog)
- Share your experience in a [talk or workshop](https://forms.gle/YpUvifDNLG6E56Cy9)


These are some ideas; if you want to contribute to our mission in another way, contact the team on Discord or send us an email at info@somosnlp.org.


🙌 Thank you so much for your support! 🙌
