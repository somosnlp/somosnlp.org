## 📚 Data collection and annotation campaigns

- [FineWeb2](https://somosnlp.org/blog/fineweb-2), led by Hugging Face: Rate the educational quality of texts in your language from 1 to 5. Help is still needed for the following languages: Asturian, Catalan, Chavacano, Basque, Galician, Guarani, Nahuatl, and Portuguese.

<div class="flex justify-center">
    <img src="/images/blog/fineweb_post.png" alt="Multilingual FineWeb" width="300">
</div>

- [INCLUDE v2](https://somosnlp.org/blog/include), led by EPFL: Share multiple-choice exams from your country. We are especially looking for exams in low-resource languages or on topics related to a country's culture (e.g., literature or history exams, driving tests, etc.), but all exams are welcome.

<div class="flex justify-center">
    <img src="/images/ilustraciones/undraw_Traveling_re_weve.svg" alt="Multilingual FineWeb" width="300">
</div>

- [#Somos600M collection campaign](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/13k0FZvkco2KmxKFoflWoftpN6jWfcIYIpCb5PZx_950/edit), led by SomosNLP: Share datasets (primarily for evaluation but also for training) in Spanish or languages of LATAM or Spain. We would like ["La Leaderboard"](https://huggingface.co/spaces/la-leaderboard/la-leaderboard) to be as inclusive as possible.

<div class="flex justify-center">
    <img src="/images/eventos/240301_somos600m.jpg" alt="Somos600M" width="500">
</div>

- [LATAM-GPT collection campaign](), led by LATAM-GPT: Share links to Spanish-language websites from your country to help us train an inclusive model.

<div class="flex justify-center">
    <img src="/images/patrocinios/LatamGPT.png" alt="LATAM-GPT" width="300">
</div>

*I am the Language Lead for FineWeb2, part of the core team of INCLUDE v2, lead of #Somos600M, and a collaborator on LATAM-GPT. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or want to get more actively involved.*
