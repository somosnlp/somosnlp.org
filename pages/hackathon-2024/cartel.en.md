To encourage different skills within our community, this year we want to give you the opportunity to design the hackathon poster. The hackathon goals are creating a quality Spanish corpus that includes the varieties of this rich language spoken by 600M people and creating a leaderboard that allows us to evaluate LLMs in Spanish. Show your most artistic side and help us create the poster!

## 🎨 How to participate

1. Design a poster (or several)
2. [Send it to us](https://forms.gle/iJrKZtBXvnAk5Ji38) before February 4th
3. We will publicly acknowledge all submissions (if you want, of course)
4. If you win, your poster will be the image of the 2024 hackathon!

## ✅ What the poster must include

- Dimensions: 1280 × 720 minimum with a 16:9 aspect ratio (horizontal)
- Title: #Somos600M
- Edition: Third
- Date: March 1 to 24, 2024
- Website: somosnlp.org/hackathon
- #HackathonSomosNLP
- [SomosNLP Logo](/images/patrocinios/somos_nlp.png)
- Some extra graphic element

## 📝 Notes

- Creations must be original
- The use of all types of design tools is allowed, including AI systems
- Transparency regarding the use of AI will be required
- Everyone's participation will be publicly acknowledged

## 🤔 Inspiration

- For example, in the [2023](https://somosnlp.org/hackathon-2023) edition we used Midjourney to create the "book-person"
- It may help to think that, once the hackathon poster is selected, we will need to adapt it to design the posters for the talks

We look forward to seeing your posters! 🤩
