### 🥇 NoticIA: Clickbait News Summarization

A dataset for summarizing clickbait articles in Spanish. 

The practice of Clickbait erodes public trust in digital news sources and harms the advertising revenue of legitimate content producers, who may experience a decline in their web traffic as a result. To address this challenge, we have created a corpus with 850 clickbait news articles in Spanish. Each article is accompanied by a high-quality, concise generative summary, written by human experts.

**SDG:** 8. Decent work and economic growth

**Project:**
- [Corpus: NoticIA-IT](https://huggingface.co/datasets/somosnlp/NoticIA-it)
- [Model: NoticIA-7B](https://huggingface.co/somosnlp/NoticIA-7B)
- [Demo](https://huggingface.co/spaces/somosnlp/NoticIA-demo)
- [Presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xc60K_NzUgk&list=PLTA-KAy8nxaASMwEUWkkTfMaDxWBxn-8J&index=17)

### 🥈 AsistenciaRefugiados: Legal assistance for refugees

Legal assistant for people in refugee or political asylum situations.

Spain is the third country with the highest number of asylum applications, receiving approximately more than 100,000 applications each year, and the third with the lowest number of approvals within the EU.

The main objective of this project is to facilitate the tasks of NGOs in this field and other institutions and help them obtain answers to questions (QA) related to refugee legislation in Spanish. With its refined understanding of the nuances and complexities of this legal field.

**SDG:** 10. Reduced inequalities 16. Peace, justice and strong institutions

**Project:**
- [Corpus: AsistenciaRefugiados](https://huggingface.co/datasets/somosnlp/instruct-legal-refugiados-es)
- [Model: AsistenciaRefugiados 7B](https://huggingface.co/somosnlp/gemma-7b-it-legal-refugee-v0.1.1)

### 💛 AviaciónInteligente: Using LLMs to Facilitate Navigation of the Colombian Aeronautical Regulation

Research that uses state-of-the-art language models to improve the understanding and navigation of the Colombian Aeronautical Regulation (RAC), with the goal of facilitating access to information and improving safety in civil aviation.

The "Aviación Inteligente: LLMs para Navegar el RAC" project is revolutionizing access to the Colombian Aeronautical Regulation through advanced Language Model technologies. With the collaboration of the Fundación Universitaria Los Libertadores and a team of expert annotators, we have created and labeled 24,000 curated entries in our dataset. Additionally, we have performed fine-tuning of the GEMMA 2B IT ColombiaRAC model, specifically optimized to effectively navigate through aeronautical regulations. This approach not only facilitates regulatory queries but also democratizes knowledge in the industry, making aeronautical information accessible to a broader audience and reducing barriers to entry in the field. This project is a step toward a future where access to and understanding of aeronautical regulations are simpler and more open for everyone.

**SDG:** 4. Quality education, 9. Industry, innovation and infrastructure

**Project:**
- [Corpus: Base de Datos del Reglamento Aeronáutico Colombiano](https://huggingface.co/datasets/somosnlp/ColombiaRAC_FullyCurated)
- [Model: AviacionInteligente](https://huggingface.co/somosnlp/AviacionInteligente_gemma-2b-it-bnb-4bit)
- [Demo](https://huggingface.co/spaces/somosnlp/AviacionInteligente_Demo)
- [Presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IGKU1qUur2c&list=PLTA-KAy8nxaASMwEUWkkTfMaDxWBxn-8J&index=25)

### SpanishMedicaLLM & SMC: Spanish Medical Corpus

Creation of an LLM to provide health information solutions and services in LATAM.

The objective of this project is to create a large language model (LLM) for the medical context in Spanish, enabling the creation of health information solutions and services in LATAM. The model will include information on conventional, natural, and traditional medicines. A result of the project is a public dataset from the medical domain that aggregates resources from other sources, allowing the creation or fine-tuning of LLMs. The LLM's performance results are compared with other state-of-the-art models such as BioMistral, Meditron, MedPalm.

**SDG:** 3. Good health and well-being

**Project:**
- [Corpus: SMC (SpanishMedicalCorpus)](https://huggingface.co/datasets/somosnlp/spanish_medica_llm)
- [Model: SpanishMedicaLLM](https://huggingface.co/somosnlp/spanish_medica_llm)

### RecetasDeLaAbuel@: Recipe corpus from Spanish-speaking countries

The 'RecetasDeLaAbuel@' corpus is a tribute to all our grandparents who taught us how to cook. It is the largest and most complete collection of recipes in Spanish from Spanish-speaking countries.

Our mission is to create a Spanish-language AI that gathers recipes from Spanish-speaking countries and helps improve our relationship with food preparation and cooking. The ultimate goal is to build an intelligent cooking assistant specific to the Spanish language.

**SDG:** 3. Good health and well-being
