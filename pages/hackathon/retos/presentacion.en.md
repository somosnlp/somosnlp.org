Congratulations on making it this far — all that's left is to present your great project to the world!

How to do this?
1. Create a demo
2. Record a 5-minute video
3. Optional: write a paper

Below we share more details and tips to maximize the visibility of your project 🚀

---

## 🖼️ Creating a demo

Create a demo of your project in a HuggingFace Space so everyone can interact with your model. Additionally, the best demos will receive an extension of ZeroGPU time sponsored by Hugging Face!

*Until May 31 | max 0.5 pts*

- If it's your first demo, we recommend using Gradio as it's simpler.
- Create the demo directly in a Space at hf.co/somosnlp-hackathon-2025 and iterate there.
<!-- - You can use `Nvidia T4 - small` GPUs sponsored by Hugging Face. -->
- What to include in the demo: project motivation, impact, future ideas, Sustainable Development Goal number if applicable, link to the dataset and model used, team members, everything you consider necessary to promote your project 🤗
- Create a clear and intuitive demo.

<details>
<summary>Incentives</summary>

- Required to consider the project as finished and qualify for prizes
- Adds up to 0.5 pts to your team's total score
- The best demos will receive an extension of ZeroGPU time sponsored by Hugging Face

</details>

<details>
<summary>Resources</summary>

- Inspiration: Check the "trending Spaces" or "Spaces of the week" at hf.co/spaces
- Docs: [Gradio docs](https://www.gradio.app/docs)
- Notebook: [How to create a demo with Gradio](https://somosnlp.org/recursos/tutoriales/06_demos_con_gradio)
- Video tutorials: [Here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q0t1bNoa0tI&list=PLTA-KAy8nxaB-HA79tlOTRl496_XIlJta) you have tutorials for creating demos using Gradio, Streamlit, and Flask.

</details>

## 🎥 Recording a 5-minute video

Record a 5-minute video presenting your project, upload it to Drive, and share it with mariagrandury [at] gmail [dot] com

The recordings will be viewed live with the jury — we recommend that at least one person from the team tries to attend the live session in case questions arise. Good luck! 🤗

*Submission until June 1 | max 0.5 pts*

<details>
<summary>Incentives</summary>

- Adds up to 0.5 pts to your team's total score
- Required by Mistral to award credits to the winning team
- Required to consider the project as finished and qualify for prizes

</details>

<details>
<summary>Guide</summary>

Content:
- The video duration must be a maximum of 5:00 minutes (strict maximum)
- The presentation content is free — you can focus on what you believe **has the most value in your project**
- In general, the following outline is recommended: motivation, methodology, results, analysis, conclusion, and future steps

Format:
- Record the screen during a Zoom or Teams meeting (recording is prohibited in Google Meet) with (ideally) all team members present
- All people present must speak
- The video cannot be edited — share the recording directly
- Confirm that audio was recorded in addition to the video
- Upload the recording to Drive and share it with mariagrandury [at] gmail [dot] com

Inspiration:
- We invite you to watch the project presentations from the 2024 edition in [this playlist](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cBDzM0CAwpw&list=PLTA-KAy8nxaASMwEUWkkTfMaDxWBxn-8J&index=14&t=1260s)

</details>

## 📝 Optional: writing a paper

With the help of researchers, write a paper presenting your project and submit it to a Workshop at an international NLP conference.


<details>
<summary>Incentives</summary>

- Gain research experience and visibility
- If your paper is accepted, you'll have the opportunity to travel to present it in person!

</details>

<details>
<summary>Resources</summary>

- We recommend the workshop where Diana Galván, Chair of the LatinX in NLP 2024 Workshop, shared many tips: ["Abstract writing workshop"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0f-wLobIOps&list=PLTA-KAy8nxaASMwEUWkkTfMaDxWBxn-8J)
