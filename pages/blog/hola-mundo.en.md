SomosNLP is the community of Spanish-speaking students, professionals, and researchers democratizing NLP in our language. Our goal is to create and share resources that enable and accelerate the advancement of NLP in Spanish.

## Why?

NLP research is centered on English and neglects the particular challenges of other languages.

💡 There are more than 570M Spanish speakers.

Let's give our widespread (and beautiful) language fair representation in the field of NLP.

## How?

* Creating datasets
* Training and fine-tuning models
* Offering free and open-source education
* Organizing talks, workshops, panels, and hackathons
* Reading and discussion groups

## And above all...

🚀 Sharing experience and resources in the Discord community.
