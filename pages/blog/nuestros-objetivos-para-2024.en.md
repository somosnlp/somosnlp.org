Today on Discord, a very active community member asked me what the community's goals were for 2024, so here are a couple of ideas. Suggestions are welcome!

1. Create a high-quality instruction corpus that represents the varieties of Spanish spoken by 600M people, as well as the co-official languages 💛

2. Standardize the evaluation of LLMs in these rich languages, enabling progress analysis and comparison of available models ✅ 

3. Offer you greater support and visibility (because you are creating incredible projects!) 🚀

Who's in? somosnlp.org/hackathon

## We continue with our mission

SomosNLP is an open and passionate community of Spanish speakers committed to a crucial mission: democratizing Natural Language Processing in our languages. Through the creation and promotion of **open-source** resources, we aim to drive the advancement of NLP in Spanish and the co-official languages.

In recent years, we have seen growth in available resources in Spanish, but there is still a long way to go. Open-source contributions are revolutionizing AI, and we need to keep up with developments in English. Worldwide, there are more than 599 million Spanish speakers (native proficiency + limited competence + Spanish language students) according to the 2023 Instituto Cervantes Yearbook -- **we are 7.5% of the world's population!**

Furthermore, the fact that Spanish is an official language in **21 countries** gives us unparalleled richness. How do you say you love something? Qué guay, copado, chévere, bacán, chido, padre, bárbaro... AI systems need to understand and use all the expressions that make up our colorful language and its varieties.

## The third edition of our hackathon arrives

Returning to the goals for 2024, we present our major annual event, the #HackathonSomosNLP.

After two editions in which we developed projects focused on the Sustainable Development Goals with "classic" Transformers (2022) and with LLMs (2023), this year's focus is on datasets for training inclusive instruction models, and on techniques for evaluating them correctly. **We need models that understand and speak the varieties of Spanish of the 600M Spanish speakers, and we need a standardized way to evaluate and compare these models.**

There are many ways to contribute: participating in the hackathon, sharing knowledge as a speaker, offering mentorship, sponsoring the event, or helping with technical logistics. All support is welcome!

## Happy 2024, see you in March!

SomosNLP is your community. We want to hear your ideas and suggestions to support you in your projects. Join us on Discord!

We look forward to seeing you at our next meetup. Happy New Year -- let's make 2024 a memorable year for NLP in Spanish!
