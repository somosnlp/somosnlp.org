With our [Hackathon #Somos600M](https://somosnlp.org/hackathon) we want to promote the creation of inclusive databases that represent the diversity of the 600 million Spanish-speaking people.

We promote the development of projects with social impact in various areas and on the occasion of International Women's Day (8M) we are launching a call to collect feminist texts that can be used to implement projects on information about laws and policies in different countries, data on the gender gap, support for victims, etc.

### 👀 What type of texts are we interested in?
- Laws and policies from different countries on gender equality and gender-based violence
- Web pages explaining different currents of feminism
- Studies and statistics on the gender gap in various countries and contexts
- Projects and initiatives that apply data to minimize gender inequality
- Any other feminist text

### 🙌 How can you share them with us?
- Reply to our [call on Twitter](https://twitter.com/SomosNLP_/status/1766124788911685958)
- Send us an email at info@somosnlp.org
- Share it in our community on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/my8w7JUxZR), especially in our "Diversidad" channel

Thank you very much! 💜
