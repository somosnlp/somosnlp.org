## How to support the initiative

- **🔥 Participate in the annotation**: Rate the educational quality of each text from 1 to 5 and contribute to the creation of a large, high-quality multilingual corpus.
    1. Create a Hugging Face account (if you don't have one yet): Sign up for a free account at https://huggingface.co/join.
    2. Visit the [annotation space](https://data-is-better-together-fineweb-c.hf.space/) (log in with your Hugging Face account).
    3. Explore the available datasets and select the language you would like to annotate.
    4. Carefully read the annotation guide before starting, as this ensures consistency across all contributions. You can find it in the bottom-left corner of the annotation interface.
    5. Start annotating:
        - Rate the educational quality of each text.
        - Use Argilla's filters and bulk actions to work efficiently.
        - Look for patterns to speed up your workflow.

    3. If it is, you can offer your help in the corresponding chat channel and share the initiative on social media.

- **🌟 Share the initiative**: Help more people in your community learn about the initiative.
    1. Take a look at the [communications kit](https://huggingface.co/spaces/data-is-better-together/leads-content) that the Hugging Face team has prepared — it includes ideas for social media posts and images.
    2. Feel free to translate and customize the content as you see fit.
    3. Share it on social media! You can mention the Language Lead for your language and/or the SomosNLP community (as for me, I will share all the posts I receive from any of the languages of LATAM and Spain).

## Information and help

We are at your disposal through the initiative's chat:
    1. Join the initiative's chat, where we will share updates and answer questions. You can join using your Hugging Face account here: [Rocket Chat](https://huggingface.co/spaces/HuggingFaceFW/discussion).
    2. Join the channel for the languages you speak and say hello 👋
    3. Mention your Language Lead to ask any questions.

Both the Hugging Face team and the *Language Leads* are at your disposal for any questions or suggestions.

Additionally, you can also contact us on [SomosNLP's Discord](https://discord.com/invite/my8w7JUxZR).
