We are 600M Spanish speakers, let's give our widespread and rich language fair representation in the field of NLP. Participate in the Hackathon SomosNLP, an international online competition whose main goal is to **create diverse and open NLP resources in Spanish and co-official languages**.

The best part? EVERYONE can collaborate!

## Previous editions

This year we celebrate the fourth edition — are you curious about the results from previous years?

### Winning projects

#### Hackathon SomosNLP 2024: #Somos600M

The three winning projects are:
- 🥇 NoticIA: Clickbait News Summarization
- 🥈 AsistenciaRefugiados: Legal assistance for refugees
- 🥉 TraductorInclusivo: Text rewriting using inclusive language

And the community's favorite project is:
- 💛 AviaciónInteligente: Navigation of the Colombian Aeronautical Regulation

Special mention to the projects:
- 👏 ThinkParaguayo: Discover Guaraní culture
- 👏 LenguajeClaro: Administrative language simplification
- 👏 BERTIN-ClimID: BERTIN-Base Climate-related text Identification

And the corpora:
- 📚 SMC: Spanish Medical Corpus
- 📚 RecetasDeLaAbuel@: Recipe corpus from Spanish-speaking countries
- 📚 LingComp_QA: A computational linguistics educational corpus in Spanish
- 📚 KUNTUR: Political Constitution of Peru 1993
- 📚 Province identification and summaries from the Rural Spanish Oral and Sound Corpus
