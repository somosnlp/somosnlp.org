Understand all the key concepts and architectures of the state of the art in NLP and apply them to practical cases using one of the most popular libraries in this field: [Hugging Face](https://github.com/huggingface). Regardless of your current knowledge, you will finish the course comfortably discussing Word Embeddings, Sequential Models, Attention Mechanisms, Transformers, and Language Modeling.

Join more than 10k students! 🚀

➡️ You can find both the workshop recordings and the slides and notebooks [here](https://somosnlp.org/nlp-de-cero-a-cien).

## Schedule
The course is divided into 7 sessions that were broadcast live every two Tuesdays at 18h CET starting July 13. Depending on your current level, you can join the course at any session.

- Jul 13: Introduction to NLP and Word Embeddings
- Jul 27: Sequential Models (RNNs and LSTMs)
- Aug 10: Transformer Architecture
- Aug 24: Transformers and Transfer Learning
- Sep 7: Transformers and Text Generation
- Sep 21: Transformers and Language Modeling
- Oct 5: NLP Demos with [Spaces](https://huggingface.co/spaces)

Each session lasts 30 minutes with 10 extra minutes dedicated to answering attendees' questions.

Did you miss a session? No worries!

- We upload the recordings to this [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLBILcz47fTtPspj9QDm2E0oHLe1p67tMz).
- In our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/somosnlp/nlp-de-cero-a-cien) you can check all the course materials and extra resources.
- You can ask your questions in the #nlp-de-cero-a-cien channel of our Slack community. Update: we are now on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/my8w7JUxZR), we continue answering your questions in the #ayuda channel.
